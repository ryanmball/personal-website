About

Hello, my name is Ryan. I am a Denver-based software engineer and recent graduate of Actualize Coding Bootcamp. At the end of 2020 I decided it was finally time for a career change and I took the leap to become a software engineer. I am loving every second of it, and my desire to learn and grow has been reignited.

My previous career as a corporate accountant helped me develop many valuable skills and revealed my affinity for solving complex problems. I learned the value of flexibility and accountability and that I always want to take pride in my work. I thrive in environments where I can collaborate on challenging problems whose solutions have an impact.

Outside of work my greatest passion is rock climbing, but I also enjoy reading sci-fi and playing strategic board games.
